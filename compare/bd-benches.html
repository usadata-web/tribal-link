How to read these comparisons

Bench size shows the raw count of BD/Capture/Proposal/Sales staff mapped per tribe. Mindset distribution shows the prior-employer-type mix (LARGE/SMALL/ANC/GOVT/INTERNAL). Function mix shows the operational shape of each BD organization. Longevity shows the senior-vs-rank-and-file tenure asymmetry — a signal for layoff exposure. Treat these as comparative scaffolding, not absolute headcount — coverage rate varies by tribe (typically 60-85% for top-tier ANCs, lower for smaller tribes with less LinkedIn presence).
