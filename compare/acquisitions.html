The big picture

The 12-month research window through May 2026 produced 5 acquisitions across 4 tribes and 3 CEO transitions. Cherokee Federal is the most aggressive M&A actor (2 acquisitions in 2 months via Sovereign Capital growth arm). ASRC's Coinstar acquisition was the largest single deal (entire new business segment). The single most distinctive single-tribe story is BSNC's ICE-shareholder-divestment governance crisis — the first major ANC governance dispute over federal customer mix.
